## Activity Labels
##### During the test

* `WALKING` (`1`): Subject is walking
* `WALKING_UPSTAIRS` (`2`): Subject is walking up a staircase
* `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS` (`3`): Subject is walking down a staircase
* `SITTING` (`4`): Subject is sitting
* `STANDING` (`5`): Subject is standing
* `LAYING` (`6`): Subject is laying down
